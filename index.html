Last Message

How This Works 3 Steps
1
Write & encrypt your message
Set a strong password and encrypt your message. A file downloads to your device, your encrypted message.
2
Send the encrypted file to your beneficiaries now
Email them the file today. They cannot read it, it's encrypted. Without the password it's just random characters. The encryption is the time-lock.
3
Keep the password secret while you're alive
Store the password in a sealed physical envelope, with your lawyer, or in a safe. Your beneficiaries find it only after you're gone. They come back here, paste the file contents, enter the password. Message unlocked.
This is exactly how every professional digital will service works: Safewill, Trust & Will, Tomorrow. The encryption is the protection, not the timing. Simple, trustless, no middleman.
